📖 About SmartLearn AR

SmartLearn AR is an AI-powered educational platform designed to make learning easier, interactive and enjoyable. Students can explore Mathematics, Science, Geography, History and many more subjects with the help of Artificial Intelligence and Augmented Reality technology.

Creates quizzes automatically for practice.

Personalized Learning

Adapts content according to student performance.
